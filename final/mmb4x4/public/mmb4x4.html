Welcome!

We are a club centered in Mid-Michigan, origionally founded in 1995. We are looking to expand and have a great time with friends and family at mudbogs and the Silver Lake Sand Dunes. Please take a look around our website and Facebook page. Also please feel free to contact us if you have any comments, questions, or suggestions.

Our Mission

To "build and test the best off-road vehicles to ever grace the mud bogs of the mid-Michigan area and the Silver Lake Sand Dunes."
